Privacy Policy

About the App

Toddler Games is an educational application designed for toddlers and preschool children. The app helps children learn basic concepts such as animal names, counting numbers, alphabet letters, and shapes in a fun and interactive way.

We respect the privacy of our users, especially children, and are committed to protecting it.

Information Collection and Use

Toddler Games does not collect, store, or share any personal information from users.

The app does not request or collect:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Location data
  • Photos or media files
  • Device contacts
  • Any other personal data

Children’s Privacy

Toddler Games is designed for children under the age of 5.

We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

If you believe that a child has provided personal information, please contact us and we will immediately remove such information.

Third-Party Services

This application does not use third-party services that collect personal user information.

Internet Connection

The app can function without requiring users to provide any personal data.
